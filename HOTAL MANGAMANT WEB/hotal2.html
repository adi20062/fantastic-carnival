About Hotel Manang Mant

Our Story

Established in 2015, Hotel Manang Mant has been a beacon of hospitality in the beautiful Manang region. Located at the heart of the Annapurna Circuit, our hotel offers an authentic Himalayan experience with modern comforts.

Our name "Manang Mant" reflects our deep connection to the local culture and our commitment to preserving the natural beauty of this magnificent region while providing world-class accommodation to travelers from around the globe.
